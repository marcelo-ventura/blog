Beginning

As a crypto investor and enthusiast, I’d be unhappy to not study and analyze its market.

After finishing this work, I was very tired and wanted to do a quick project which could make me happy. So I called a friend in Rio and invited him to make a project submission to the annual excellent event SER (International Seminar of Statistics with R) which is hosted and created by UFF (Universidade Federal Fluminense).

He replied me and we talked about mutual interests among cryptos, and we’ve decided to work on a Poster submission about Crypto modeling.

Despite all limitations (including page limits) and the distance between Rio and Brasília we finished everything in 5 days.
